"About Us!"

More info

Early 2024, a group of souls from the eastern part of Hyderabad thought of organizing Sri Sri Sharadiya Durgotsav on a bigger platform, making it reachable to a large number of people around the city. The dream was big and challenging indeed as we did not have the needed backup other than the willingness to do something bigger! With the help of a bunch of well-wishers, we started growing as we found a lot of other like-minded souls around who also dream similar to what we do! Today, we are a big MBS family which is registered under Telangana Society Registration Act 2001 with more than one hundred and fifty individual families, and still growing!
